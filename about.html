"DESIGNER UNDEFINED"

DESIGNER UNDEFINED is the worldwide style platform. The technological platform, which was founded in 2XXX to bring trust to the sneaker community, has subsequently grown to provide clothes and accessories from the world's best modern, avant garde, and luxury labels. The company's unique stance between the main and secondary marketplaces allows it to surface styles from the past, present, and future, offering real items to over 20 million members in 164 countries.

" HOW DOES DESIGNER UNDEFINED WORK?"

On DESIGNER UNDEFINED, sellers, including stores and boutiques, put their products for sale, while buyers browse over a million listings.

Resale items, including shoes, must first be shipped to us for verification to guarantee they are authentic and as stated. We will mail them to you after they have been validated. If we discover that the product is not real or as advertised, we will tell you and issue a complete refund.

Retail goods, such as clothes and accessories, are pre-verified and shipped straight from our retail and boutique partners.

"Instant" items are pre-verified and validated before being shipped directly to you from our facilities.
